About the series

This cache series is designed to put your technical knowledge to the test. Each cache coordinates can be discovered using technology.

Some of the caches may require you to utilize 3rd party tools not provided or sanctioned by geocaching.com or groundspeak. Every attempt has been made to keep these puzzles secure, however you should use at your own risk!
