Welcome to Houston’s Custom Framing & Fine Art

 

We are the complete resource for all custom framing needs for the Pacific Northwest and beyond.  In addition, we are pleased to offer high quality digital services, which include scanning, printing and photo restoration Our gallery represents an array of nationally and internationally recognized artists including gallery owner, Thomas M. Houston, Russell Chatham, Erin Lee-Gafill,  Yuji Hiratsuka and Richard Spare among others. Our mission is to provide our customers with optimum value by offering the highest level of service, finest quality materials, workmanship and design.   From the smallest, simplest child’s drawing to works by the Masters, family photographs, heirloom treasures, needlework,  Aunt Martha’s spoon collection or any other kind of special project, we give every piece our fullest attention.  Our moulding selection is one of the largest in the Pacific Northwest and ranges from natural woods to 23k gold – contemporary to period reproductions, and everything in between – from the simple to the sublime. Owner Thomas Houston has been framing professionally for 28 years and is also a nationally exhibited artist.  With a B.F.A. in painting as well as having achieved the  M.C.P.F.(Master Certified Picture Framer) designation by the Professional Picture Framer’s Association, Thomas has the unique ability to merge artistic creativity with technical ability.
